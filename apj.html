Life Story

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He was the 11th President of India and is also known as the "People's President." Dr. Kalam was the chief scientist at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).

Dr. Kalam made significant contributions to India's missile program and nuclear tests. He believed that every individual has a unique quality, and it is important to recognize it and work to bring out the best in oneself.

He always said, "Dreams are not what we see when we sleep, dreams are those that don't let us sleep." His hard work and discipline inspired the nation in many ways.
