Life Story

Ratan Tata was born on December 28, 1937, in Bombay (now Mumbai), India. He is a prominent Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and the former chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group. Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded its footprint globally, acquiring prestigious brands such as Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel.

Ratan Tata is known for his leadership qualities, vision, and his commitment to corporate social responsibility. He has been instrumental in transforming the Tata Group into a global conglomerate, focusing on innovation, sustainability, and ethical business practices.
